Installation Instructions:
1. Download the speedbreaker.apk file from github to your computer.
2. Connect the android device to the computer. Be sure to enable USB debugging on the device. To do that go to Settings>Developer Option.
3. Copy the speedbreaker.apk file to any folder in your android device.
4. Open the speedbreaker.apk file in your android device and follow the instructions to install the app.

Usage Instructions:
1. Find the installed app (speedbreaker) in your android device and open the app.
2. Change the speed tolerance if required. Default is set to 5mph.
3. Press the START button. Based on your motion, the app will display the nearest speedlimt and your current speed. Ensure that your GPS is on.
4. If your speed exceeds the speedlimit by the speed tolerance, the app will produce a notification sound. Ensure that your phone is not in silent mode.
5. The notification sound is the sound set on your android device. This can be changed.
6. Press the STOP button to stop the app.
